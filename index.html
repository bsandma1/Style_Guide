<H1> - Main Heading

  • margin: 50px;
  • font-family: sans-serif;

  • width: 80%;
  • margin: 50px auto;
  • background-color: white;
  • border: 4px solid black;
  • border-radius: 20px;
  • padding: 15px 30px 30px 30px;
  • box-shadow: 8px 8px 2px 2px dimgray;
